Chambers USA, the prestigious, independent referral directory of the legal industry, is produced by London-based Chambers and Partners and annually ranks the leading U.S. firms and attorneys based on in-depth research and client and peer interviews. Individual attorneys are evaluated on their legal knowledge and experience, ability and effectiveness, and client service.

In the Chambers Global Guide 2026, Bradley is listed for Privacy & Data Security: Healthcare, and three of the firm’s attorneys are highly ranked for Privacy & Data Security: Erin Jane Illman (Privacy & Data Security: Cybersecurity), Amy Leopard (Privacy & Data Security: Healthcare), and Eric Setterlund (Privacy & Data Security: Cybersecurity; Privacy & Data Security: Healthcare).

In the Chambers USA High Net Worth Guide 2025, Bradley is listed in Band 1 in Alabama for Private Wealth Law, and five partners are ranked for Private Wealth Law. The firm has been ranked for the last nine years.

The 2026 edition of The Best Lawyers in America® lists 422 attorneys from across all offices, including 101 attorneys who are listed in Best Lawyers: Ones to Watch in America. Now in its 32nd year, The Best Lawyers in America is one of the most highly regarded attorney referral publications in the country.

The 2026 edition of Best Law Firms includes rankings in 75 national practice areas and 127 metropolitan areas. Rankings are based on client evaluations, peer reviews from leading attorneys, and review of additional information provided by law firms as part of the formal submission process. To be eligible for a national or metropolitan ranking, a law firm practice area must have at least one lawyer in that practice ranked in The Best Lawyers in America®.

The selection process includes independent research, peer nominations and peer evaluations to determine the honorees.

Benchmark Litigation is the definitive guide to America’s leading litigation firms and attorneys based on examination of recent casework and extensive interviews with litigators and clients. Benchmark Litigation determines rankings through peer reviews and case examinations, and is the only publication that focuses exclusively on U.S. litigation.

The 2024 edition of Benchmark Litigation’s Labor & Employment Guide ranked seven partners as “Labor & Employment Stars” and listed Bradley as a “Highly Recommended” firm in Alabama and Tennessee in the area of labor and employment.

The guide is based on research and extensive interviews conducted with litigators, dispute resolution specialists and their clients to identify the leading firms and practitioners across a range of issues related to labor and employment.
